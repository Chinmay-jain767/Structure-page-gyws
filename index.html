Structure

Structure

We will have two pools of members in the program. One will be the members of GYWS and others will be the volunteers and interns of GYWS.


our teams


Fundraising

This team shall be responsible to explore various funding/investment options for the business under PRAYAS. This team will also bring funds for PRAYAS trust from the government or other funding organizations.

Business Model Development

This team will build a strategy and plan of action for the businesses after their proposal has been reviewed by the review team.

Marketing

This team will create the content for the Website and all other Social Media Platforms of PRAYAS. They will work on reaching the target audience of PRAYAS through Digital platforms.

Executive

This team will coordinate with the point of contact in the businesses and also be responsible to monitor that they follow the action plan designed by the business development team.

Finance

This team will be responsible to handle the finances of the PRAYAS program. The following are the responsibility of the finance team: To manage the payments and keep a record of all the transactions made under PRAYAS To track the expenses made to run the office To get the disbursements done in a particular time period after the approval of the concerned GB member To review the accounts Review the finances of the enterprises and train the entrepreneurs to manage their own finances.
